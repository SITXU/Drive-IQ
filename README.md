🚗 DriveIQ — Intelligent Automotive Platform
Your AI-powered smart car companion

DriveIQ is an advanced automotive intelligence platform that combines Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, and Predictive Analytics to make vehicles safer, smarter, and more reliable.

It doesn’t just monitor your car — it understands, predicts, and assists in real time.

🌟 What DriveIQ Does

DriveIQ brings together three powerful AI systems into one seamless platform:

🤖 AI Fault Diagnosis Chatbot

Talk to your car like you would to a mechanic.

Describe any issue in plain language:

“My car makes a rattling noise when braking”

DriveIQ analyzes the problem using an AI model trained on real vehicle manuals and responds with:

Likely causes
Estimated repair cost
Suggested next steps

It can even help you book a service instantly.

👁️ Driver Behavior Monitoring

DriveIQ watches out for you while you drive.

Using real-time computer vision, it detects:

Driver drowsiness
Mobile phone usage
Seatbelt compliance
Driving patterns

You get a behavior score and instant alerts to improve safety.

📊 Predictive Maintenance Engine

DriveIQ predicts problems before they happen.

By analyzing vehicle data like:

Mileage
Engine temperature
Oil levels
Brake wear
Service history

It can warn you:

“High risk of brake failure in next 2000 km”

This helps you avoid breakdowns and reduce repair costs.

📊 Smart Dashboard

Everything is visualized in a clean, interactive dashboard:

🚘 Vehicle Health Score
⚠️ Risk Alerts
📈 Maintenance Timeline
🧠 AI Predictions
👤 Driver Behavior Analytics

You always know the exact condition of your vehicle — at a glance.

🏗️ How It Works

DriveIQ uses a modern, scalable architecture:

Frontend: React.js dashboard
Backend: Node.js API gateway
Database: MongoDB (vehicle + user data)
AI Engine: Python FastAPI microservice
AI Engine Includes:
LangChain-powered chatbot
OpenCV + MediaPipe vision system
Scikit-learn predictive models

All components work together in real time using APIs and sockets.
